About CloudSentinel MarketLink

CloudSentinel MarketLink is a digital marketplace project developed under CloudSentinel Technologies. It is designed to connect buyers, sellers, SMEs, service providers, farmers, students, and local businesses across Nigeria.

The platform serves as a business bridge where users can upload products or services, discover opportunities, communicate with sellers, and arrange safe physical transactions.

🎯 Our Purpose

To help SMEs and individuals gain digital visibility and connect with more customers.

🛡️ Safety First

We promote safe trading by encouraging users to meet physically and inspect items before payment.

🌍 Local Business Growth

The project supports local commerce by giving small businesses a simple online marketplace presence.

🚀 Future Vision

CloudSentinel MarketLink can later grow into a real operational marketplace with chat, verification, and escrow features.
